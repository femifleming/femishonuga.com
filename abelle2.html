arma17

I don’t know much about the history of micro house and rominimal music. I love the way it sounds, it’s deep and textural. Long textures and ambiance, impossible architectures and hypnotic beats. Tribal drums micro sounds that excite performance spaces like impulses, filling the room with an eerie after tone in waiting for the next kick. This is not a review on microhouse, I love the way it sounds and sometimes it’s indescribable. It makes my booty bounce and thats all that matters, and abelle is an amazing selector. And Arma is a great label. I wish I could be at shows like this. It’s true underground. With concrete walls and nerds who like their brain itched by glitchy beats.
