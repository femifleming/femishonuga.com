Sour, Sour Face, 5.0%, 6.8/10, alright wack graphic design but it’s cheeky for what it taste like. I like the lightness of the kiwi strawberry flavor, it’s not super sweet or synthetic, however you do kind of get that acidic flavor in the back of your throat after a while, which isn’t terrible but was a downside for me. A smaller can of this would have been perfect as a day treat:)
