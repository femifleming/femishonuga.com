Rutland Vermont

On Friday, Sept 5th 2025 Enid and I went to Rutland Vermont to stay with some of her friends from a stonecarving residency she took out there. They were having a birthday party with a lamb that had been killed, skinned and placed on a rotating pole to cook over coal and wood in the outdoors for at least a day. The fresh lamb was too gamey for Enid but I thought it was perfection. We visited Hill Farmstead Brewery, their Saison was to die for. We also visited Bread and Puppet, a radical theatre creating paper mache sculptures since the 1960s. They were having an amazing show. These are pics from this weekend.
